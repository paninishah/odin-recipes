Strawberry Milkshake!

Description

This easy strawberry milkshake is made with fresh strawberries, blended with milk, and your favorite strawberry ice cream for a classic creamy and refreshing summer treat.

Ingredients

  • 13 fresh strawberries
  • 4 scoops strawberry ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Â½ cup ice
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream, or to taste
  • 2 teaspoons chocolate sauce, or to taste

Steps

  1. Blend strawberries, ice cream, milk, honey, and vanilla extract together in an electric blender. Blend in ice.
  2. Pour into tall glasses. Top with whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate sauce. Enjoy!
