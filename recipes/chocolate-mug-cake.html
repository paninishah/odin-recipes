Eggless Chocolate Mug Cake!

Description

This easy chocolate eggless mug cake is made with flaked almonds and no egg. It is ready in a few minutes and perfect if you're dying for something sweet. Serve dusted with confectioners' sugar and topped with ice cream or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Â½ tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon sliced almonds

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, milk, cocoa powder, olive oil, and baking powder in a large mug until well combined. Stir in chocolate chips and almonds.
  3. Place mug in the microwave and cook until cake has risen by 50 percent, 30 seconds to 2 minutes.
  4. Garnish with the sliced almonds, and dig in!
